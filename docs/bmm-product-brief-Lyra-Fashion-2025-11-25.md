# Product Brief: Lyra Fashion

**Project Name:** Lyra Fashion  
**Date:** 2025-11-25  
**Author:** Bibek  

## Executive Summary

**Vision:** Create a direct-to-consumer fashion platform that showcases and sells modern women's clothing produced in our factory, building brand presence while establishing meaningful customer relationships and bypassing traditional retail channels.

**Core Mission:** Establish Lyra Fashion as a recognized brand for modern women's clothing through an online platform that allows customers to discover, explore, and purchase our designs directly from the source.

---

## Initial Vision & Context

**What sparked this idea:**
- Need to create an online presence for Lyra Fashion brand
- Desire to showcase factory's modern women's clothing to potential buyers
- Goal to directly reach customers (direct-to-consumer model)
- Opportunity to control brand narrative and customer experience

**Business Context:**
- Established clothing factory with production capabilities
- Focus on modern women's clothing
- Seeking to establish direct relationship with end consumers
- Moving beyond wholesale/B2B to B2C model
